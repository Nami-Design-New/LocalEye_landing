Exploring the Industrial and Economic Participation Policies

Implementation and Prospects

Background of the report:

Building on the long-established concept of "Economic Offset," the Industrial and Economic Participation concepts hold significant recognition today.

The Industrial and Economic Participation Policies for the Military and Civil sectors in Saudi Arabia aim to enhance the Government's Procurement Value. Importantly, these Policies align with the objectives of the Saudi Vision 2030 and other concurrent National Strategies.

LocalEyes observes a growing adoption of these Policies. Drawing upon our experience and extensive knowledge of the Saudi economy, the Report provides an outlook and insights with regards to these Policies and their impact.
